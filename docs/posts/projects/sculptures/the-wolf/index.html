The Wolf

Title
Description
2019

Design and realization of the life size metal statue of a male wolf. Realized from a mix of different scrap pieces and black steel. The sculpture was a work on commission, Berlin, July and August 2019 The wolf is now located in the Spreewald, a rural area near Berlin where the wolves are coming back to.
